ALLU ARJUN

South indian Movies Stylish Super Star

Allu Arjun is an Indian film actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema. After playing as a child artist in Vijetha and as a dancer in Daddy, Arjun made his adult debut in Gangotri. Arjun then appeared in Sukumar's debut film Arya. Allu Arjun completed his college in Chennai. His maternal uncle Chiranjeevi is one of the top actors in the Telugu industry. He is the grandson of late comedian Padmashree Allu Ramalingaiah. He is also known as a stylish star.
